Farmers choose agricultural pesticide products from GRAMSEVA AGRI SOLUTION to protect crops from harmful insects, diseases, and pests that hinder their growth. With superior quality and scientifically backed solutions, our products deliver confidence and reliability.

Insecticides

Insecticides kill or repel pests and vectors of diseases in crops and livestock, safeguarding agricultural productivity and public health.

Fungicides

Fungicides control fungal diseases, protecting plant health and improving yields by inhibiting harmful pathogens like rusts, mildews, and rots.

Herbicides

Herbicides inhibit weed growth in fields, lawns, and gardens, preventing yield loss and landscape damage caused by unwanted plants.

PGR

Plant Growth Regulators (PGR) optimize crop growth and development by altering physiological processes to improve plant height, fruit setting, and ripening.
